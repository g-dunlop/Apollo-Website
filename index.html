Who we are

Accredited by Cambridge English and the International House World Organisation and part of Apollo Education and Training, one of Vietnam's largest private language providers, Apollo Teacher Training has over 20 years' experience in training new and experienced language teachers in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City.

Whether you're looking to teach for the first time, or are an experienced teacher looking to take your career to the next level, find out how our team of highly-exerienced trainers can help you to get industry-leading, internationally-recognised qualifications in as little as 4 weeks.

Entry-level Training

The Cambridge CELTA is the world's most recognised and respected entry-level TEFL course. Go from zero to job-ready in as little as 4 weeks.

Experienced Teachers

Take your teaching to the next level with the Cambridge DELTA, or help others improve their teaching by taking the Cambridge Train the Trainer course.

Young Learner Teachers

The International House Certificate in Teaching Young Learners and Teenagers is for those looking to specialise in teaching children from 4-16.

Meet the Team

Our trainers are all accredited by the University of Cambridge English Language Assessment. They are all very experienced English teachers and IH tutors have typically worked in a variety of settings and locations. All our trainers are committed to teacher development and have an active, ongoing interest in this field.

Jon

Graeme

Michael

Jon Rose (CELTA/DELTA)

Jon Rose is a CELTA/Delta trainer with 30 years’ experience working as a teacher and teacher trainer in Spain, Egypt, the UAE, the US, Australia and Vietnam in academic institutions and private language schools. He has been a Delta trainer since 2004 and CELTA trainer since 1998. He is a Delta Module 2 and Module 3 Assessor.

Graeme Dunlop (CELTA/IHCYLT)

Graeme is from the UK and has worked in the UK, Russia, Spain, Poland and Vietnam. He has taught General English, one – on – one, business English, Cambridge Main Suite and IELTS exam preparation courses as well as a wide variety of Young Learner courses. He has worked as a senior teacher, academic manager and examiner. As well as CELTA training, Graeme also works as a trainer for the International House Certificate in Teaching Young Learners and Teenagers (IHCYLT).

Michael Newby (CELTA/IHCYLT)

Hailing from the UK, Michael has been working in ESL since 2006. He has worked in numerous countries as a teacher, teacher-trainer and manager. Michael has trained on CELTA courses around the world, including Turkey, Dubai, Italy, Malaysia, the USA, South Africa, Botswana, Canada and Germany. Michael is also a Cambridge CELTA Assessor.

Laurie Campbell Miller (CELTA/IHCYLT)

Laurie is from Scotland and has been working in ELT since 2004. She has taught in numerous contexts; VYL,YL, Immersion, EAP, 1-2-1, ESP, Business, Online and TOEFL/Cambridge/IELTS exam prep. She has experience in a variety of roles including management and Content development. She has worked in several different countries such as Indonesia, South Korea, Italy, Saudi Arabia, Japan, The UAE, England and most recently, Vietnam.
